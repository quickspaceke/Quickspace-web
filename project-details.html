Mobile Application Redesign

A mobile delivery app designed to provide a seamless, fast, and efficient experience for users in Nigeria, with a primary focus on competing with existing giants like Glovo and Chowdeck. By offering a more intrusive, user-centered design and integrating multiple categories, TukShopp aims to redefine how Nigerians order food, groceries, pharmaceuticals, and other market items. The app features a delivery and pickup service, with the flexibility of scheduling and a smooth order tracking experience.

A user-first interface designed for TukShopp, a fast-growing app in Jos, Nigeria that enables grocery, food, pharmacy, and market item deliveries directly to homes. The goal: a seamless, multi-category shopping experience that’s intuitive for all users.

01. The Challenge

TukShopp needed a unified platform catering to diverse services—groceries, pharmacies, market traders, and restaurants—while ensuring ease of use for less tech-savvy users. Flexibility, reliability, and real-time order tracking were essential.

02. The Solution

Developed an intuitive design system with clean navigation and bright, engaging visuals.

Built seamless onboarding + KYC and multi-outlet vendor management flows.Designed a stacked order tracking interface that clearly shows progress: New → Accepted → Ready → Out for Delivery
