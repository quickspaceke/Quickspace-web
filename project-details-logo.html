Concept Development, 3D Logo Design, Brand Identity Styling

At Quickspace, 3D logo branding is more than design — it’s visual strategy. This case study brings together logos we crafted for Miller Beauty Spa, Bits & Pieces (Fashion), and Miller Training Center, each requiring unique appeal, yet sharing the same high-quality, digital-first approach.

Experience how Quickspace delivers premium 3D logo branding across industries — from beauty and wellness to fashion and education.

01. Miller Beauty Spa

Objective: Reflect luxury, femininity, and self-care in a sleek, modern format.

Design Insight: We used soft curves, glowing gold textures, and a beauty mark motif to give it a graceful, premium salon vibe.

Result: A brand that looks spa-ready across signage, packaging, and digital.Now screams premium branding on social media and advertisement. This Gives a modern and high end appeal attracting the right customers.

02. Bits & Pieces – Fashion Brand

Objective: A chic fashion identity appealing to young, urban women.

Design Insight: We built a 3D logo with fabric textures and interwoven lettering to reflect layering and diversity in fashion.

Result: Perfect for product tags, online stores, and influencer placements.

“Logos are a graphic extension of the internal realities of a company.”

Saul Bass

03. Miller Training Center

Objective: Build trust and professionalism while remaining approachable to youth.

Design Insight: A geometric, shield-like 3D form symbolizing growth, strength, and structured learning.

Result: A flexible logo that works across branches, uniforms, and certificates.
